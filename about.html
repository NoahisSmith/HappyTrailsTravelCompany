About Happy Trails Travel

Personal, stress-free planning — backed by 20+ years of experience.

Travel planning made personal

For more than two decades I've helped travelers turn their ideas into trips they actually look forward to. Whether it's a long-awaited family vacation, a honeymoon, a destination wedding, or a getaway just because — I handle the research, the booking, and the little details that so often cause stress.

You bring the dream; I bring the experience, the connections, and the know-how to make it happen smoothly.

What working together looks like

1. We talk it through

Share your destination ideas, dates, budget, and what matters most to you. No pressure, no obligation.

2. I build your plan

I research options and put together an itinerary tailored to you — flights, stays, activities, and the details in between.

3. You travel happy

Everything is booked and organized. You enjoy the trip while I stay available if anything comes up along the way.
